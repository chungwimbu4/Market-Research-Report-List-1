<p><p>The car cleaner market consists of three primary types: dry type, wet type, and dry-wet mixing type. </p><p>Dry type cleaners primarily utilize powders or sprays that absorb dirt and grime without water, making them ideal for quick cleanups. Wet type cleaners involve liquid solutions that require water for effective cleaning, often providing deeper penetration into dirt and stains. Dry-wet mixing types combine both methods, using a liquid and a powder for a comprehensive cleaning approach, ensuring flexibility and effectiveness for various cleaning needs.</p></p>
