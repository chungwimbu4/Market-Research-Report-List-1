<p><p>The dental excavators market mainly features two types: round and spoon excavators. Round excavators are designed with a rounded edge, ideal for removing decay from cavity preparations and soft tissue. Spoon excavators, on the other hand, have a spoon-shaped design, allowing for effective removal of debris and carious dentin in deeper cavities. Both tools are essential for enhancing dental procedures, ensuring precise cleaning and shaping of tooth structures, thus contributing to improved patient outcomes and oral health.</p></p>
