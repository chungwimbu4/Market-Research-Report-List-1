<p><p>The LED exit signs market is divided into two main types: electrical exit signs and non-electrical exit signs. Electrical exit signs utilize LED technology for illumination, providing energy efficiency and longer lifespans. They are often connected to a power source for constant operation. In contrast, non-electrical exit signs rely on photoluminescent materials or other passive mechanisms for visibility, requiring no electricity and functioning effectively during power outages. Both types ensure safety and compliance with building codes.</p></p>
