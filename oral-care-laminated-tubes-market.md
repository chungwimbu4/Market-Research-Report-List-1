<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oral Care Laminated Tubes market is primarily categorized into two types: ABL (Aluminium Barrier Laminates) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminates). ABL tubes consist of layers that include aluminum, providing superior barrier properties against moisture, light, and oxygen, ensuring product stability and extending shelf life. PBL tubes, made from various plastics, offer flexibility and a lightweight option, often utilized for cost-effective packaging. Both types serve the oral care industry by ensuring product integrity and facilitating user convenience.</p></p>
