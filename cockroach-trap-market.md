<p><p>The cockroach trap market can be categorized into three main types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are pre-packaged units that attract roaches with enticing food while delivering a toxic substance. Granular baits consist of small granules that target cockroaches through ingestion, often used in spaces like kitchens and gardens. Gel baits are viscous substances applied in crevices and corners, providing a highly palatable option for cockroaches, ensuring effective control and eradication of infestations.</p></p>
