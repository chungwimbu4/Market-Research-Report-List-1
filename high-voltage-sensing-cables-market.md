<p><p>The High Voltage Sensing Cables market is primarily categorized into single-mode fiber and multi-mode fiber types. Single-mode fiber cables use a single light pathway, offering greater distance and lower signal attenuation, making them ideal for long-range high voltage monitoring. In contrast, multi-mode fiber cables allow multiple light paths, providing higher bandwidth over short distances, suitable for localized applications. Both types enable effective temperature and strain sensing in high voltage environments, ensuring safety and efficiency in electrical infrastructure.</p></p>
