<p><p>Flat-film extrusion lines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and operation. Horizontal lines, on the other hand, are designed for wider applications, providing better film thickness control and surface quality. Each type serves specific production needs, with vertical lines being favored for compact setups, while horizontal lines excel in high-output scenarios requiring versatile film properties.</p></p>
