<p><p>Rower machines come in several types, each offering unique resistance mechanisms. Magnetic type machines use magnets to create smooth, adjustable resistance, ideal for quiet workouts. Air type rowers rely on airflow for resistance, providing a dynamic and challenging experience that intensifies with speed. Water type models mimic rowing on water, utilizing water resistance for a natural feel. Hydraulic type rowers use pistons for compact, affordable options, suitable for home use. Each type caters to different user preferences and fitness goals.</p></p>
