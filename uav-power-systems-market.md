<p><p>The UAV power systems market can be broadly categorized into uniaxial and coaxial systems. Uniaxial systems typically feature a single rotor configuration, providing straightforward design and simplicity for applications requiring stability and efficiency. In contrast, coaxial systems utilize two rotors stacked on top of each other, enhancing lift and maneuverability while maintaining a compact design. Each type offers distinct advantages, making them suitable for various UAV applications in commercial, industrial, and military sectors, based on performance and operational requirements.</p></p>
