<p><p>The Data Center Busway market is primarily categorized into two types: 3-Phase 4-Wire and 3-Phase 5-Wire systems. The 3-Phase 4-Wire busway provides three phases of power and a neutral wire, making it suitable for balanced loads typical in data centers. In contrast, the 3-Phase 5-Wire busway incorporates two additional ground wires, enhancing safety and reliability by minimizing electrical disturbances. Both types are designed to efficiently distribute power while maximizing flexibility and scalability in data center environments.</p></p>
