<p><p>The water pollution control equipment market can be categorized into two main types based on contaminant nature: particulate contaminants and chemical contaminants. Particulate contaminant control equipment targets solid particles such as sediments, debris, and microorganisms, utilizing methods like filtration and sedimentation. Meanwhile, chemical contaminant control equipment addresses dissolved substances like heavy metals, pesticides, and industrial chemicals through processes such as chemical precipitation, adsorption, and advanced oxidation. Both types are essential for ensuring clean, safe water across various applications.</p></p>
