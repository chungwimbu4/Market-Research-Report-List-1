<p><p>UAV surveying using laser lidar technology primarily encompasses two market types: near-infrared laser and water-penetrating green light types. Near-infrared lasers are utilized for terrestrial applications, offering high-resolution data for vegetation and topography mapping. In contrast, water-penetrating green light lidar is specifically designed for underwater surveying, allowing for the measurement of submerged features and aquatic environments. Both types enhance the capabilities of UAVs in various applications, including environmental monitoring, construction, and resource management, contributing to improved data accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
