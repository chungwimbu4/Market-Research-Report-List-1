<p><p>The aerospace landing gear market is primarily segmented into two categories: main landing gear and nose/tail landing gear. The main landing gear supports the bulk of the aircraft's weight during landing and taxiing, providing stability and strength. In contrast, nose or tail landing gear is crucial for steering and balance, facilitating ground maneuverability. Both types are essential for safe landings and takeoffs, influencing aircraft design, performance, and maintenance strategies within the aviation industry.</p></p>
