<p><p>The polyurethane spray equipment market is primarily divided into two types: high pressure and low pressure spray equipment. High pressure spray equipment operates at elevated pressures, enabling a fine atomization of polyurethane materials, ensuring a smooth finish and efficient coverage. This type is commonly used for industrial applications. In contrast, low pressure spray equipment uses lower pressures for a more controlled application, minimizing overspray and waste, making it ideal for residential or smaller-scale projects. Both types cater to distinct user needs and preferences.</p></p>
