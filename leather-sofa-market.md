<p><p>The leather sofa market is categorized into several types, primarily focusing on the quality and processing of the leather used. Aniline leather is dyed with soluble dyes, retaining its natural characteristics, offering a soft and luxurious feel but less durability against stains. Semi-aniline leather combines aniline and pigmented leather, providing a balance of natural appearance and resistance to wear and tear. Other types include pigmented leather, which is treated for enhanced durability and color consistency, catering to various consumer preferences and usage conditions.</p></p>
