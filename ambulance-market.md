<p><p>The ambulance market is categorized into Type A, Type B, Type C, and Rapid Response Vehicles. Type A ambulances are van-based, designed for basic life support (BLS) and patient transport. Type B ambulances are larger, offering advanced life support (ALS) with enhanced medical equipment. Type C ambulances, or modular ambulances, are built on a truck chassis, providing maximum space and advanced care capabilities. Rapid Response Vehicles are smaller, agile units for quick medical intervention, often used in emergencies to stabilize patients before transport.</p></p>
