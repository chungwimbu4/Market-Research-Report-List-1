<p><p>The Sedan and Hatchback Wheel Speed Sensor market is primarily categorized into Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type sensors. Hall Type sensors use a magnetic field to detect wheel speed, providing precise measurements essential for vehicle stability and anti-lock braking systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric Type sensors rely on changes in magnetic flux to generate voltage signals, translating wheel motion into speed data. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety, performance, and efficiency in modern automobiles.</p></p>
