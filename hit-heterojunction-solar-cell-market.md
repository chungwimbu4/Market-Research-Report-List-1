<p><p>The HIT (Heterojunction) solar cell market is primarily segmented into monofacial and bifacial cell types. Monofacial cells capture sunlight from one side, typically offering higher efficiency in limited space. In contrast, bifacial cells harness sunlight from both sides, utilizing reflected light from surfaces beneath them, thus increasing energy output. This dual functionality makes bifacial cells appealing for large-scale installations, while monofacial cells are favored for residential and smaller applications where space is constrained. Both types benefit from advances in technology and efficiency.</p></p>
