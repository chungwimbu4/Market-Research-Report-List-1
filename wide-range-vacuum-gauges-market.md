<p><p>The Wide-Range Vacuum Gauges market is categorized into two types: indirect measurement and direct measurement. Indirect measurement gauges utilize equations and properties of gases to infer vacuum levels, often providing broader operational ranges. Direct measurement gauges, on the other hand, offer precise readings of vacuum levels through physical measurements, such as pressure changes in a diaphragm or other sensing mechanisms. Both types serve various industries, ensuring accurate vacuum assessments for processes requiring specific pressure conditions.</p></p>
