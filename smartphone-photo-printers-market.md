<p><strong>The Smartphone Photo Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Dye Sublimation Printer</li><li>Inkjet Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone photo printer market features two main types: thermal dye sublimation printers and inkjet printers. Thermal dye sublimation printers use a heating process to transfer dye onto photo paper, producing vibrant, long-lasting images with smooth gradations. They are compact, quick, and ideal for on-the-go printing. Inkjet printers utilize liquid ink sprayed onto paper, allowing for high-resolution prints and versatility in media types. While inkjet printers require more maintenance and drying time, they offer greater flexibility for various printing needs.</p></p>
