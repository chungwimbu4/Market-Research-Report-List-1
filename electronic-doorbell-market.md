<p><p>The electronic doorbell market encompasses various types, including wired and wireless options. Wired doorbells connect directly to the home's electrical system, providing reliable operation but requiring professional installation. Wireless visible doorbells are battery-operated and typically feature a camera, allowing homeowners to see visitors remotely. In contrast, wireless invisible doorbells, often integrated into smart home systems, remain discreet and can send alerts to smartphones without external hardware. Each type offers unique advantages to cater to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
