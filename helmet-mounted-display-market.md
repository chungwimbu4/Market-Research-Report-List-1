<p><strong>The Helmet-Mounted Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slide-on HMD</li><li>Discrete HMD</li><li>Integrated HMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Helmet-Mounted Display (HMD) market includes three main types: Slide-on HMD, Discrete HMD, and Integrated HMD. Slide-on HMDs are designed to attach to existing helmets, providing enhanced display capabilities without significant modifications. Discrete HMDs are standalone devices that maintain a low profile, offering essential information while keeping the user's line of sight unobstructed. Integrated HMDs are built directly into the helmet, offering a seamless experience with advanced features, but potentially increasing weight and complexity. Each type serves different use cases and user preferences.</p></p>
