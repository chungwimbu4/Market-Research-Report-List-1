<p><p>Punching pliers are categorized into four primary market types: CNC, Mechanical, Pneumatic, and Hydraulic. CNC punching pliers use computer numerical control for precise and automated punching, ideal for high-volume production. Mechanical types rely on manual force, offering simplicity and portability. Pneumatic pliers utilize compressed air for efficient, rapid operation, making them suitable for medium-scale tasks. Hydraulic punching pliers leverage hydraulic pressure, providing powerful punching capabilities for thick materials, often utilized in heavy-duty industrial applications. Each type caters to specific needs and applications.</p></p>
