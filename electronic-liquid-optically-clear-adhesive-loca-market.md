<p><p>The Electronic Liquid Optically Clear Adhesive (LOCA) market is divided into two main types: Resistive OCA and Capacitive OCA. Resistive OCA is primarily used to bond displays in devices requiring touch sensitivity, providing a solid optical performance with reliable durability. Conversely, Capacitive OCA is designed for touchscreens that detect electrical changes, ensuring high clarity and responsiveness. Both types are crucial in electronics for enhancing display performance and durability in smartphones, tablets, and other optical applications.</p></p>
