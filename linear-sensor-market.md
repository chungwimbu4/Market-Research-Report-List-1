<p><p>The linear sensor market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Capacitance sensors detect changes in capacitance to measure position or level. Eddy current sensors utilize electromagnetic fields to gauge distance or displacement in metallic objects. Photoelectric sensors employ light beams to identify position or movement. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to measure distance or detect objects. Other sensors may include technologies like optical linear encoders or magnetic sensors, broadening the scope of linear measurement solutions available in the market.</p></p>
