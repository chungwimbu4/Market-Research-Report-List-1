<p><p>IT Asset Management (ITAM) markets can be categorized into two main types: cloud-deployed and on-premises solutions. Cloud-deployed ITAM utilizes cloud technology to provide flexible, scalable, and often subscription-based services, enabling organizations to manage their assets remotely and efficiently while benefiting from automatic updates and reduced infrastructure costs. Conversely, on-premises ITAM involves software installed directly on an organization's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. Each type addresses distinct business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
