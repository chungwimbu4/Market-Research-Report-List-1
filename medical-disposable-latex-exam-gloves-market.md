<p><p>The medical disposable latex exam gloves market is divided into two main types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves, coated with cornstarch, facilitate easy donning and removal, making them a popular choice among healthcare providers. However, concerns about allergic reactions and contamination risks are associated with their use. Non-powdered gloves, on the other hand, lack this coating, reducing the risk of allergic reactions and offering a safer option, particularly in sensitive environments, although they may be slightly more challenging to put on.</p></p>
