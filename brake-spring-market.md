<p><strong>The Brake Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Progressive Spring</li><li>Linear Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake spring market includes progressive and linear springs, each serving distinct functions. Progressive springs offer variable resistance, increasing stiffness as compression rises, ideal for applications requiring adaptable braking force. Linear springs provide consistent resistance throughout compression, ensuring predictable and uniform braking performance. Progressive springs are favored in dynamic systems needing variable response, while linear springs suit applications demanding steady force. Both types are essential in automotive and industrial braking systems, catering to specific performance and safety requirements.</p></p>
