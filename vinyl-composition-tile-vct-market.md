<p><p>Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) is a durable, cost-effective flooring solution commonly used in commercial and institutional settings. Single-layer VCT consists of a uniform thickness and is primarily made from vinyl and limestone, offering straightforward installation and maintenance. In contrast, multi-layer VCT features additional layers that enhance durability, aesthetics, and performance, often incorporating different materials for improved resistance to wear and tear, making it suitable for high-traffic environments.</p></p>
