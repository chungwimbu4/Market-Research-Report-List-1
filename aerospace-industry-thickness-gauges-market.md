<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes thickness gauges primarily categorized into ultrasonic, magnetic, and other types. Ultrasonic gauges measure material thickness by using high-frequency sound waves, ideal for non-destructive testing of various materials, including composites and metals. Magnetic gauges rely on magnetic flux to ascertain thickness, commonly used for ferrous materials. Other types encompass methods like eddy current and laser-based gauges, offering versatility for diverse applications in aircraft manufacturing and maintenance, ensuring structural integrity and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
