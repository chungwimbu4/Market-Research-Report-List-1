<p><p>Polyurethane sealants in the construction market are categorized into two main types: single component and multi component. Single component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture, making them convenient and easy to apply. Multi component sealants require mixing of two or more components before application, offering enhanced performance and customization options for specific applications. Both types provide excellent adhesion, flexibility, and durability, making them ideal for sealing joints, cracks, and providing weatherproofing in construction projects.</p></p>
