<p><p>The laser die cutting machine market for labels and flexible packaging includes two main types: rotary laser die cutting machines and flatbed laser die cutting machines. Rotary machines utilize a rotating drum to cut continuous materials, enhancing speed and efficiency for high-volume production. In contrast, flatbed machines use a stationary bed to precisely cut various shapes from sheets, offering flexibility for smaller batches and intricate designs. Both types are pivotal in modern packaging and label production, providing precision and versatility.</p></p>
