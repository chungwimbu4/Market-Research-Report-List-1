<p><p>The riveting equipment market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic riveters use compressed air to drive rivets, offering speed and portability, making them ideal for lightweight applications. Hydraulic riveters utilize fluid pressure for greater force, suitable for heavy-duty tasks and thick materials. Electric riveters provide precision and consistency, powered by electric motors, making them user-friendly and efficient for small to medium-sized projects. Each type serves distinct needs based on power, application, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
