<p><ul><li>Chip-on-film Underfills</li><li>Flip Chip Underfills</li><li>CSP/BGA Board Level Underfills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfills in the semiconductor market enhance durability and reliability by filling gaps between components and substrates. Chip-on-film underfills are used to bond chips to flexible substrates, ensuring mechanical stability. Flip chip underfills support face-down chip connections, providing excellent thermal and electrical performance. CSP/BGA board-level underfills are employed for chip-scale packages and ball grid arrays, offering protection against thermal stresses and enhancing solder joint reliability. Each type serves unique applications, improving overall device performance and longevity.</p></p>
