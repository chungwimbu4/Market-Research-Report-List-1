<p><p>The organic tobacco market consists of four main types based on curing methods. Flue cured organic tobacco is dried using heated air, enhancing sweetness and aroma. Air cured organic tobacco is naturally dried in the open air, producing a milder flavor. Sun cured organic tobacco relies on direct sunlight for drying, resulting in a unique taste profile. Fire cured organic tobacco involves exposing leaves to smoke from burning wood, imparting a distinctive smokiness. Each type caters to different consumer preferences in the organic tobacco segment.</p></p>
