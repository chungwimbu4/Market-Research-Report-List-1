<p><ul><li>Hot Launch</li><li>Cold Launch</li><li>Concentric Canister Launch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vertical Launch System (VLS) Cell market comprises three primary types: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. Hot Launch systems fire missiles directly from their launchers, utilizing onboard propulsion, enabling rapid response. Cold Launch systems eject missiles from a canister using gas or a booster, allowing for safer operations and minimizing heat exposure. Concentric Canister Launch integrates multiple projectiles within a single canister, optimizing space and enhancing versatility in missile deployment. These variations cater to different operational needs and tactical requirements.</p></p>
