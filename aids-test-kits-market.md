<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The AIDS Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AIDS test kits market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Antibody Tests** detect antibodies produced by the immune system in response to HIV, typically using blood or oral fluid samples.</p><p>2. **RNA (viral load) Tests** measure the actual amount of HIV RNA in the blood, providing insights into the viral load and infection status.</p><p>3. **Antibody-antigen Tests** identify both antibodies and the p24 antigen, a marker of early HIV infection, allowing for earlier detection compared to antibody-only tests. Each type serves distinct diagnostic and monitoring purposes in managing HIV/AIDS.</p></p>
