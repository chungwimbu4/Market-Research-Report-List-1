<p><p>The polyethylene (PE) foam market is categorized into three main types: High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE foam is known for its strength and durability, making it ideal for applications requiring structural integrity. LDPE foam is softer, more flexible, and often used in cushioning and packaging. LLDPE foam combines the properties of both HDPE and LDPE, offering enhanced stretchability and impact resistance, suitable for diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
