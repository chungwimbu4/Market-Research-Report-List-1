<p><p>Trunnion block-mounted ball valves are versatile flow control devices used in various industrial applications. The market is segmented into two main types: full bore and reduced bore. Full bore valves have a larger internal diameter, allowing unrestricted flow, making them ideal for applications requiring maximum flow capacity. Reduced bore valves, with a smaller diameter, create more turbulence but can be advantageous for size and weight reduction in piping systems. Each type caters to specific operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
