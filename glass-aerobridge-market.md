<p><p>The Glass Aerobridge market features two main types of elevation systems: electromechanical and hydraulic. The electromechanical elevation system utilizes electric motors and mechanical components to raise and lower the bridge, offering precise control and energy efficiency. In contrast, the hydraulic elevation system relies on fluid pressure to operate, providing smooth and powerful movements, often suited for heavy-duty applications. Both systems are crucial for enhancing passenger experience by facilitating seamless boarding and alighting from aircraft in modern airports.</p></p>
