<p><strong>The Reflex Type Photoelectric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Retroreflective Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Reflective Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflex type photoelectric sensor market consists of retroreflective and diffuse reflective sensors. Retroreflective photoelectric sensors use a reflector to bounce the emitted light back to the sensor, detecting objects based on the interruption of this light. In contrast, diffuse reflective sensors detect objects by measuring the light reflected directly off the object's surface without the need for a separate reflector. Both types are widely used in automation, packaging, and safety applications, offering reliable and effective sensing solutions.</p></p>
