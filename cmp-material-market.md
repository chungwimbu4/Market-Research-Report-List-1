<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) material market is primarily divided into two key components: CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasive materials used to polish semiconductor surfaces, ensuring uniformity and flatness. CMP slurries, on the other hand, consist of chemical compounds and abrasive particles that facilitate the planarization process by removing excess material from the wafer surface. Both components are essential for achieving high-quality finishes in semiconductor manufacturing and other advanced material applications.</p></p>
