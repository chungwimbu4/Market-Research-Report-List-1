<p><p>The resorbable adhesion barrier market is categorized into three formulations: film, gel, and liquid. Film formulations offer a thin, flexible layer that adheres to tissues, providing a barrier against adhesions while dissolving over time. Gel formulations present a viscous material that conforms to the surgical site, delivering effective adhesion prevention and promoting healing. Liquid formulations are typically injectable, allowing for precise application in hard-to-reach areas, ensuring thorough coverage and barrier formation to minimize postoperative adhesions.</p></p>
