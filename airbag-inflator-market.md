<p><p>The **Airbag Inflator Market** comprises three main types: **Pyrotechnic Inflators**, **Stored Gas Inflators**, and **Hybrid Inflators**. Pyrotechnic inflators use chemical reactions to generate gas rapidly, ensuring quick deployment. Stored gas inflators rely on pre-compressed gas, offering smoother deployment with less heat. Hybrid inflators combine both methods, using a small pyrotechnic charge to release stored gas, balancing speed and efficiency. Each type caters to specific safety needs, with hybrid inflators gaining popularity for their balanced performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
