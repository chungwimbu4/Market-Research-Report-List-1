<p><p>The pipe bending machines market primarily consists of two types: hydraulic pipe bending machines and CNC pipe bending machines. Hydraulic pipe bending machines utilize hydraulic pressure to bend pipes efficiently, ensuring high precision and flexibility for various pipe sizes. CNC pipe bending machines, on the other hand, employ computer numerical control technology for automated bending processes. This allows for enhanced precision, repeatability, and the ability to execute complex bending designs, making them ideal for industries requiring high-volume production and intricate designs.</p></p>
