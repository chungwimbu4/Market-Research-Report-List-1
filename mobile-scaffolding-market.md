<p><p>The Mobile Scaffolding Market can be categorized into two main types: Distance Connection and Variable Distance Connection. Distance Connection refers to scaffolding systems that maintain a fixed distance between supporting elements, providing stability for projects at a set height. In contrast, Variable Distance Connection allows for adjustable spacing between components, offering flexibility for varying project requirements and heights. These distinctions cater to different construction needs, ensuring safety and efficiency in diverse building environments.</p></p>
