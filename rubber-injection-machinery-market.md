<p><p>Rubber injection machinery is categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their orientation and operational efficiency. Vertical type machinery utilizes a space-saving design, allowing for efficient production in confined areas, making it ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, horizontal type machinery offers greater accessibility and flexibility for larger production runs, facilitating easier part removal and maintenance.
