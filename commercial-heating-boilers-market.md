<p><strong>The Commercial Heating Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-condensing Boilers</li><li>Condensing Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial heating boilers market primarily consists of non-condensing and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiency, releasing exhaust gases without capturing residual heat, making them suitable for older infrastructures. In contrast, condensing boilers are designed to maximize efficiency by utilizing flue gas heat to preheat incoming water, resulting in reduced energy consumption and emissions. As energy standards tighten, the trend is shifting towards condensing boilers due to their superior efficiency and eco-friendliness.</p></p>
