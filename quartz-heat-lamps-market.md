<p><strong>The Quartz Heat Lamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave</li><li>Medium Wave</li><li>Long Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Heat Lamps market is categorized into three types based on their wavelength emissions: short wave, medium wave, and long wave. Short wave lamps emit high-intensity infrared radiation, providing deep tissue heating and quick results, making them suitable for therapeutic and industrial applications. Medium wave lamps offer a balance between penetration and surface warming, often used in drying processes. Long wave lamps generate lower intensity heat, ideal for applications requiring gentle warmth, such as skin treatments and heating objects.</p></p>
