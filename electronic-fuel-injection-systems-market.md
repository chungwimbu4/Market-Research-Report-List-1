These systems are widely used in gasoline and diesel engines across passenger vehicles, commercial vehicles, and motorcycles.</p><p>The Electronic Fuel Injection Systems Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing demand for fuel-efficient vehicles, stringent emission regulations, and advancements in automotive technology.
