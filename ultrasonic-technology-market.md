<p><p>The ultrasonic technology market is primarily divided into two types: detection ultrasound and power ultrasound. Detection ultrasound focuses on the use of ultrasonic waves for sensing and imaging applications, such as medical diagnostics, non-destructive testing, and industrial inspections. In contrast, power ultrasound refers to high-intensity ultrasonic waves utilized in applications like cleaning, welding, and processing materials. Both segments harness the principles of ultrasound, but serve distinct purposes across varied industries, enhancing efficiency and precision in their respective applications.</p></p>
