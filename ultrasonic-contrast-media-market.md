<p><p>The Ultrasonic Contrast Media market includes iodine-based and barium sulfate compounds, each serving distinct imaging purposes. Iodine-based contrast agents enhance visualization in vascular imaging and organ studies, leveraging their ability to absorb ultrasound waves. In contrast, barium sulfate compounds are primarily used in gastrointestinal imaging, providing clarity during procedures such as barium swallows and enemas. Both types improve diagnostic accuracy by increasing the contrast between different tissues, facilitating better detection and assessment of medical conditions.</p></p>
