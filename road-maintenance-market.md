<p><p>The road maintenance market involves various methods for enhancing pavement durability and performance. The Sol-Gel Process utilizes chemical solutions to create protective coatings, improving resistance to wear and environmental factors. The Intercalating Method incorporates materials between existing pavement layers, enhancing structural integrity. The Blending Method mixes additives directly into asphalt or concrete, optimizing properties for better longevity. Lastly, the Packing Method focuses on compacting materials to improve load distribution and reduce cracking, ensuring a more resilient road surface.</p></p>
