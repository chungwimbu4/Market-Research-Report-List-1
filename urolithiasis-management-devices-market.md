Stone removal devices, like graspers and baskets, assist in extracting stones directly from the urinary tract. Ureteral stents are used to maintain urine flow and reduce complications during stone removal procedures. Together, these devices enhance the effectiveness of urolithiasis treatment, improving patient outcomes and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
