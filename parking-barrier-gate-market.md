<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Parking Barrier Gate market primarily features two types: straight and crank. Straight barrier gates provide a vertical lifting motion, ideal for high-traffic areas where quick access is needed. They offer simplicity and efficiency. Crank barrier gates, on the other hand, utilize a hinged design that swings open, making them suitable for locations with limited vertical space. Each type caters to different operational needs, with straight gates focusing on speed and crank gates prioritizing space-saving solutions while ensuring secure parking access.</p></p>
