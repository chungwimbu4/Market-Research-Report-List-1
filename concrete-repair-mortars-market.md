<p><strong>The Concrete Repair Mortars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-modified Cementitious Mortar</li><li>Epoxy-based Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete repair mortars are categorized into two primary types: polymer-modified cementitious mortars and epoxy-based mortars. Polymer-modified cementitious mortars incorporate polymers to enhance adhesion, flexibility, and durability, making them suitable for a variety of repair applications. In contrast, epoxy-based mortars utilize epoxy resins, providing superior strength, chemical resistance, and bonding capabilities, ideal for heavy-duty repairs and industrial environments. Both types cater to different repair needs, ensuring effective solutions for damaged concrete structures.</p></p>
