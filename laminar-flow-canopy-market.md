<p><p>The laminar flow canopy market is categorized into two primary types: built-in fan and fan external systems. Built-in fan canopies integrate fans within the unit, providing a streamlined design that maintains a compact footprint while ensuring effective air circulation over sterile environments. External fan systems, on the other hand, utilize separately positioned fans to achieve laminar airflow, which can allow for more flexibility in layout and maintenance. Both types are essential in various applications, including laboratories and healthcare settings, to ensure contamination-free workspaces.</p></p>
