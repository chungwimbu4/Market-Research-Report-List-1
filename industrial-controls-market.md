<p><p>The industrial controls market encompasses various systems designed for efficient process management. Distributed Control Systems (DCS) focus on controlling production processes across multiple locations, ensuring real-time monitoring and automation. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems provide centralized data collection and control of industrial processes, often over vast geographic areas. Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) bridge the gap between production and enterprise systems, optimizing manufacturing operations. Other market types include programmable logic controllers (PLC), human-machine interfaces (HMI), and industrial internet of things (IIoT) applications.</p></p>
