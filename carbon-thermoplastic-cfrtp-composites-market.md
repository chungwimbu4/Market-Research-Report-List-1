<p><p>Carbon thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites are advanced materials primarily categorized based on the type of carbon fiber precursor used: PAN (polyacrylonitrile) and pitch. PAN-based CFRTPs are known for their mechanical strength, lightweight properties, and versatility, making them popular in aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, pitch-based CFRTPs offer higher thermal and electrical conductivity, often utilized in specialized applications such as electronics and thermal insulation. Both types enhance performance while reducing weight, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
