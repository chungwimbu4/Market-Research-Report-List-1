Crystal type duplexers utilize quartz crystals for precise frequency control, offering high stability and low loss, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Ceramic type duplexers use ceramic materials for similar functions, providing good performance at a lower cost and in a compact form. Other types may include various hybrid and semiconductor technologies that cater to niche applications, expanding the versatility of microwave communication systems.</p></p>
