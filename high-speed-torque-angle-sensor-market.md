<p><p>The High-Speed Torque Angle Sensor market includes **Potentiometer Torque Sensors** and **Non-contact Torque Sensors**. Potentiometer sensors use mechanical contact to measure torque and angle, offering cost-effective solutions but with wear and limited lifespan. Non-contact sensors use magnetic or optical principles, providing higher durability, precision, and reliability, ideal for high-speed applications. Both types cater to industries like automotive, aerospace, and robotics, with non-contact sensors gaining traction due to their maintenance-free operation and suitability for demanding environments.</p></p>
