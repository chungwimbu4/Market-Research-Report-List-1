Separation Analysis involves techniques like chromatography, which segregates components in a mixture for detailed examination. Molecular Analysis encompasses methods such as spectroscopy and mass spectrometry to analyze molecular structure and interactions, essential for fields like pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and materials science.</p></p>
