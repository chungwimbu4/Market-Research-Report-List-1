<p><ul><li>Radical Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy curing resins market includes two primary types: radical and cationic. Radical type resins are cured through a photoinitiated free radical polymerization process, making them suitable for applications like inks and coatings due to their quick curing times and excellent adhesion properties. Cationic type resins, on the other hand, use cationic polymerization initiated by UV light or heat, offering superior chemical resistance and durability. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to different industry needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
