<p><ul><li>Open Isolator</li><li>Closed Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmacy isolators market is divided into two main types: open isolators and closed isolators. Open isolators provide a controlled environment with minimal contamination risks, allowing personnel to work directly with the product while maintaining an aseptic environment. In contrast, closed isolators offer a more secure barrier, protecting both the product and the operator from potential contamination, often used for handling hazardous materials. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and sterility in pharmaceutical compounding and production processes.</p></p>
