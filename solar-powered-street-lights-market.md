<p><strong>The Solar Powered Street Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar powered street lights are categorized into two main market types: standalone and grid-connected. Standalone solar street lights operate independently, utilizing solar panels to harness solar energy, storing it in batteries for nighttime illumination. This makes them ideal for remote areas without electricity. In contrast, grid-connected solar street lights integrate with the existing electrical grid, allowing them to draw power when sunlight is insufficient. This approach offers enhanced reliability and can reduce overall energy costs while promoting sustainability.</p></p>
