<p><p>Alloy nanoparticles are advanced materials with diverse applications, categorized based on solubility. **Water-soluble alloy nanoparticles** dissolve in water, making them ideal for biomedical, environmental, and aqueous-phase applications. **Oil-soluble alloy nanoparticles** are compatible with organic solvents, suited for lubricants, coatings, and oil-based systems. **Both-phase soluble alloy nanoparticles** exhibit dual solubility, enabling versatility in hybrid systems like emulsions, catalysis, and multi-phase reactions. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, driven by solubility properties and application requirements in sectors like healthcare, energy, and manufacturing.</p></p>
