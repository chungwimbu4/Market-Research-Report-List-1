<p><p>The capillary rheometer market is categorized into single-barrel and multi-barrel types. Single-barrel capillary rheometers feature one barrel for testing fluid viscosity, ideal for simple assessments of polymer flow behavior. Multi-barrel capillary rheometers, on the other hand, contain multiple barrels, enabling simultaneous tests under various conditions. This facilitates comparative analysis and more comprehensive data collection, making them suitable for complex materials or extensive product development needs. Both types are essential for understanding material properties in industries like plastics, composites, and coatings.</p></p>
