<p><p>Portable water penetration testers are essential tools for assessing water resistance in materials. The Air Pressure Difference Method measures air pressure variations to estimate water penetration, ensuring structures remain watertight. In contrast, the Static Pressure Head Method involves applying a predetermined water pressure to evaluate leakage and structural integrity. Both methods provide critical data for construction projects, helping engineers ensure compliance with water resistance standards and enhancing the durability and safety of buildings and infrastructure.</p></p>
